📐 What is Trigonometry?

Trigonometry is the study of relationships between angles and sides of triangles.

📊 Basic Trigonometric Ratios

sin θ = Opposite / Hypotenuse
cos θ = Adjacent / Hypotenuse
tan θ = Opposite / Adjacent

🧮 Important Formulas

sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
1 + tan²θ = sec²θ
1 + cot²θ = cosec²θ
